About Us

StreetPaw is a blockchain-powered initiative dedicated to providing food, shelter, and medical care for street dogs. Our mission is to ensure no dog is left hungry or homeless.

Our Token

Introducing StreetPaw Token (PAW), a cryptocurrency designed to fund our efforts to help street dogs. Every transaction helps support food, shelter, and medical aid.

  • Token Symbol: PAW
  • Total Supply: 1,000,000,000
  • Purpose: Donations, rewards, and sustainability.
